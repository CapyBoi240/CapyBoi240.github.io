Who are we?

Welcome to Moonlight Studios, an independent game development studio based in the heart of Serbia. Our passion for gaming drives us to create unforgettable experiences, exemplified by our standout titles, "Super Pet Fighter" and "Clonescape."

We were founded in 2021 by two friends who shared a profound passion for creating video games. We take pride in our distinctive style and unwavering commitment to quality. "Super Pet Fighter" and "Clonescape" are just the beginning, showcasing our dedication to crafting games that resonate with players. As we grow, we continue to explore new dimensions, pushing the boundaries of creativity.
